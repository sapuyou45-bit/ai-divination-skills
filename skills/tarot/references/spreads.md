# Tarot Spreads

Use the smallest spread that fits the question.

## single

One card for a quick lens.

Position:

- Focus

Best for: daily reflection, a stuck moment, a starting prompt.

## three-card

Three cards for a compact reading.

Positions:

- Situation
- Obstacle
- Guidance

Best for: general clarity, personal choices, creative blocks.

## decision

Four cards for comparing options.

Positions:

- Option A
- Option B
- Hidden factor
- Advice

Best for: two-path choices. Do not turn it into a deterministic yes/no verdict.

## creative

Three cards for writing and ideation.

Positions:

- Character
- Conflict
- Turn

Best for: story structure, campaign ideas, product narratives.

## project

Three cards for technical or project reflection.

Positions:

- Current system
- Technical debt
- Next step

Best for: project retrospectives, architecture tradeoffs, blocked implementation work.
