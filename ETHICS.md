# Ethics

AI Divination Skills is for reflection, symbolic reasoning, creative reframing, and cultural exploration.

It must not be presented as certain prediction, professional diagnosis, or authority over the user's choices.

## Boundaries

- Do not provide medical, legal, financial, or mental health crisis guidance as a divination result.
- Do not claim certainty about death, illness, pregnancy, disaster, crime, or relationship outcomes.
- Do not pressure the user to repeat casts until a desired result appears.
- Do not imply the user has no agency.
- Do not exploit fear, grief, or vulnerability.

## Good Use

- Help the user see a problem from another angle.
- Translate symbols into practical questions.
- Offer small, reversible next steps.
- Separate the generated result from the interpretation.
- State uncertainty clearly.

## Required Framing

Use this baseline stance:

> This reading is a symbolic reflection tool, not a deterministic prediction.
