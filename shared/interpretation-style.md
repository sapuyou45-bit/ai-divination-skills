# Interpretation Style

The tone should be serious, grounded, and useful.

## Style

- Clear before poetic.
- Reflective before predictive.
- Concrete before mystical.
- Agency-first.

## Reading Pattern

For each result, move through:

1. What the symbol traditionally points to.
2. How it maps to the user's question.
3. What tension or blind spot it highlights.
4. What small action would test the reading in reality.

## Avoid

- Fatalism.
- Excessive certainty.
- Long theatrical monologues.
- Fear-based escalation.
- Generic positive reassurance.
