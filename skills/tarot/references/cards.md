# Tarot Card Reference

Use these as concise prompts, not fixed verdicts.

## Major Arcana

| Card | Core theme | Shadow | Practical question |
|---|---|---|---|
| The Fool | beginning, risk, openness | naivety | What can start small? |
| The Magician | agency, tools, focus | manipulation | What resources are already available? |
| The High Priestess | intuition, hidden knowledge | passivity | What is known but not yet said? |
| The Empress | growth, care, creation | overindulgence | What needs nurturing? |
| The Emperor | structure, authority, boundaries | rigidity | What needs clearer rules? |
| The Hierophant | tradition, teaching, institutions | conformity | Which convention helps or limits this? |
| The Lovers | alignment, choice, values | divided motives | What choice reveals the real value? |
| The Chariot | direction, discipline, momentum | forcefulness | What needs coordinated effort? |
| Strength | patience, courage, restraint | suppression | How can pressure be met gently? |
| The Hermit | reflection, study, solitude | isolation | What needs quiet investigation? |
| Wheel of Fortune | cycles, timing, change | helplessness | What is turning without permission? |
| Justice | fairness, evidence, consequence | cold judgment | What would be balanced and accountable? |
| The Hanged Man | pause, reversal, surrender | stagnation | What changes if the view is inverted? |
| Death | ending, transition, release | clinging | What needs to end cleanly? |
| Temperance | integration, pacing, repair | dilution | What needs proportion? |
| The Devil | attachment, compulsion, constraint | denial | What bargain has become a chain? |
| The Tower | disruption, truth, collapse | avoidance | What unstable structure is being protected? |
| The Star | hope, renewal, guidance | distance | What restores faith without fantasy? |
| The Moon | uncertainty, dreams, distortion | confusion | What is unclear and needs testing? |
| The Sun | clarity, vitality, success | overexposure | What becomes simple in daylight? |
| Judgement | reckoning, calling, review | self-condemnation | What is ready to be answered for? |
| The World | completion, integration, arrival | closure anxiety | What cycle is complete? |

## Minor Arcana

Suits:

- Wands: energy, initiative, work, ambition.
- Cups: emotion, relationship, belonging, desire.
- Swords: thought, conflict, language, decisions.
- Pentacles: material reality, money, health routines, craft.

Ranks:

- Ace: seed.
- Two: choice or balance.
- Three: development.
- Four: stability or pause.
- Five: friction.
- Six: adjustment or return.
- Seven: challenge.
- Eight: effort or movement.
- Nine: culmination.
- Ten: completion or burden.
- Page: learner.
- Knight: active pursuit.
- Queen: mature receptivity.
- King: mature direction.
