# Xiao Liu Ren Positions

Use the position as a compact symbolic signal, not a final verdict.

| Index | Pinyin | English | Core signal | Practical reading |
|---:|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Da An | Great Peace | stable, slow, safe | Keep the base steady. Good for maintenance, planning, and low-risk progress. |
| 2 | Liu Lian | Lingering Delay | delay, attachment, unclear movement | Expect friction or waiting. Simplify the ask and avoid forcing speed. |
| 3 | Su Xi | Swift Joy | quick news, timely help, opening | Act while the window is open. Communicate clearly and follow up fast. |
| 4 | Chi Kou | Red Mouth | argument, speech risk, conflict | Be careful with wording. Avoid escalation and document agreements. |
| 5 | Xiao Ji | Small Fortune | modest gain, partial help | Good for small wins. Do not overextend the meaning. |
| 6 | Kong Wang | Empty Void | absence, low traction, uncertainty | Pause, verify facts, and avoid relying on weak signals. |

## Interpretation Pattern

- For action questions, map the position to pace and risk.
- For relationship questions, map it to communication and expectations.
- For project questions, map it to execution timing and coordination.
- For daily readings, give one practical focus.
