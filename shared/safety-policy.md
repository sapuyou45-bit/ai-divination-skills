# Safety Policy

Divination readings are not professional advice.

## Refuse or Redirect

Redirect the user to appropriate help when they ask for:

- Medical diagnosis or treatment decisions.
- Legal decisions or claims.
- Financial trading or investment instructions.
- Self-harm, suicide, abuse, stalking, or crisis situations.
- Certainty about death, pregnancy, crime, or disaster.

## Safer Alternative

For high-stakes topics, offer:

- A reflective framing of the user's feelings or tradeoffs.
- A list of questions to ask a qualified professional.
- A small next step that does not increase risk.

## Language Rules

Avoid:

- "This will happen."
- "The cards say you must..."
- "You have no choice."

Prefer:

- "One way to read this is..."
- "This points toward a question..."
- "A practical next step could be..."
